Suppose we want to quantify all peaks containing an immonium signature ion. We can search for this like: --scan-file xxx.mzML --msn-ion 216.043 If we wish to add the additional restriction that the fragment is tryptic (ends with a lysine or arginine), we can add their y1 ions as well. --scan-file xxx.mzML --msn-ion 216.043 147 175
